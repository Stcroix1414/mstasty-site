Who is MsTasty?

A variety streamer rocking a custom purple donut PNGtuber setup — built from scratch. Actual eyes and mouth greenscreened onto a donut. Yes it's a guy. Yes it's a donut. No further questions.

Started as a TFT grind and turned into a whole thing. Now we play whatever looks fun — TFT ranked, Windrose, League, Tarkov, Civ. The vibes are always immaculate even when the gameplay isn't.
